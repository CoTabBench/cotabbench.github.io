Evaluation Notes

  • Counting: Row & column counting — tests structural comprehension of merged cells.
  • Table QA: Question answering — joint semantic and structural reasoning over tables.
  • Hall. Detect: Hallucination detection — Data Absence & Attribution Error adversarial questions.
  • Overall is the macro-average of the three task scores. Use the Difficulty toggle above to view Easy or Hard subset scores independently.
  • MLLMs (Multimodal LLMs) received table screenshots as image input; all other models received serialized text input.
  • CoTabLLM is fine-tuned on CoTabInstruct (training splits only); no test data leakage.
